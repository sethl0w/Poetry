Sequels – squeals – whimpers – 

life comes in folds.

A dreaded man beats his dog,

the boy whimpers.




You can’t protect them all,

there is nothing we can do 

for him. 




In moments like these, 

the streets vibrate so violently.

disregard, not to care.
